Health & Safety Guidelines

Last updated: {{ now.strftime('%B %d, %Y') }}

1. General Safety

  • Follow all posted instructions and staff directions
  • Stay in designated visitor areas
  • Keep a safe distance from all wildlife
  • Do not feed or attempt to touch any animals

2. Health Considerations

  • Stay hydrated throughout your visit
  • Use sunscreen and protective clothing
  • Be aware of weather conditions and dress appropriately
  • Inform staff immediately if you feel unwell
  • It is your responsibility to inform our staff about any pre-existing health conditions or medical concerns that may affect your participation in activities

3. Adventure Activities

  • Participate only in activities appropriate for your fitness level
  • Use all safety equipment provided
  • Follow weight and height restrictions on rides
  • Secure all personal belongings during activities

4. Emergency Procedures

  • Locate emergency exits upon arrival
  • In case of emergency, follow staff instructions
  • Emergency contact number: (555) 123-HELP
  • First aid stations are marked on park maps

By booking a visit to Wednesday's Wicked Adventures, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to follow these guidelines.
